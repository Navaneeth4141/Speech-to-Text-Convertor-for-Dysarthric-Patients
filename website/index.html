About

Supporting Rehabilitation

This project addresses the communication challenges faced by patients with dysarthria—a motor speech disorder affecting articulation, voice quality, and speech intelligibility.

By leveraging state-of-the-art deep learning models fine-tuned on dysarthric speech patterns, we provide significantly improved transcription accuracy compared to conventional ASR systems.
